Our Process

1. Project Initiation

Click on the widget in the lower right corner to make an appointment. If you need any help or have any questions, you can reach us at +1 (202) 696-7219 or at info@smoothdmv.com.

2. Confirm your appointment

4 days before you will get a text to confirm your appointment, unconfirmed appointments will get canceled 48 hours before to avoid no-shows.

3. Before your appointment

Avoid exposing the treatment area to sun for three weeks before, don't wax, pluck, or use self tanning products. Shave the area the day before the appointment.

4. Day of appointment

Don't put makeup, lotion, perfume, or any other product on the treatment area day of your appointment.

5. ARRIVE ON TIME

We do not have a waiting area and we only have a 5 minute grace period. It is crucial that you arrive EXACTLY on time, otherwise we will have to reschedule your appointment.

5. During your appointment

What to expect: check in - get changed into one of our robes - treatment (don't worry, our nurses are super gentle) - payment - scheduling of next session.

6. Payment

We are very transparent about our prices, you pay per session, after session. We do offer a 10% discount if you purchase a package of 5 treatments but this is by no means required!

7. Leave a review

We want to hear from you! Whether your experience was good or great, we want to hear your honest feedback, so please please pretty please leave us a review! thx xx

8. After the session

After your appointment we recommend staying out of the sun and using SPF 30+ for three weeks, not applying any products except for soothing aloe vera for at least 24 hours, and avoiding saunas, steam rooms, and high intensity physical activity.

9. Enjoy less shaving!

After only a couple of sessions, your hair growth will slow down and you will have to shave less often - yay!

10. Thank you for your support!

Seriously, you're the best!
